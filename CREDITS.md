# Credits & Attributions 📜

**Hermes Clipper** is built on the shoulders of giants. We believe in open-source, agentic synergy, and giving credit where it's due.

## 🧠 Core Agentic Integrations
- **[Hermes Agent](https://github.com/frostmute/hermes-agent):** The brain of the operation. Without Hermes, we're just another link-hoarder.
- **[Caveman Skill](https://github.com/JuliusBrussee/caveman):** (by Julius Brussee). This project utilizes the "Caveman-speak" compression methodology to slash token costs by ~70%. Licensed under the **MIT License**. We acknowledge Julius's viral insight into linguistic token-efficiency as a foundational part of our "Contextual Nutrient" philosophy.

## 📡 The Bridge & CLI (Python Ecosystem)
- **[FastAPI](https://fastapi.tiangolo.com/):** For the lightning-fast, asynchronous bridge server.
- **[Uvicorn](https://www.uvicorn.org/):** The ASGI server powering our bridge.
- **[Pydantic](https://docs.pydantic.dev/):** For robust data validation and settings management.
- **[BeautifulSoup4](https://www.crummy.com/software/BeautifulSoup/):** For surgically stripping "Digital Fat" (HTML junk) before it hits the Agent.
- **[Requests](https://requests.readthedocs.io/):** For reliable web scraping in CLI mode.

## 🏡 The Garden (Obsidian Ecosystem)
- **[Obsidian API](https://github.com/obsidianmd/obsidian-api):** For seamless vault integration.
- **[Esbuild](https://esbuild.github.io/):** For high-speed bundling of our TypeScript plugin.

## 💡 Inspirations & Philosophy
- **Second Brain / Zettelkasten:** The underlying philosophy of organized, cross-linked knowledge.
- **"Agent-Growth" Philosophy:** Inspired by the realization that AI agents should not just *process* data, but *consume* it to improve their own contextual awareness.
