# Hermes Clipper Manifesto

## The Problem
Humans are great at finding links, but terrible at filing them. We hoard tabs. We have folders named "To Read" that haven't been opened since 2022. Traditional clippers just move the mess from the browser to the vault.

## The Solution
**Hermes Clipper.** We don't just "clip". We **delegate**.
The clipper is the scout; Hermes is the librarian. 

## The Suite
1. **The Hand (CLI):** Raw power.
2. **The Brain (Skill):** Procedural intelligence.
3. **The Bridge (FastAPI):** Secure gateway.
4. **The Scout (Extension):** One-click capture.
5. **The Garden (Plugin):** The interface of truth.

## Project Philosophy: "Sharp & Sarcastic"
- **Efficiency over Ceremony:** No bloated UIs. 
- **Agent-First:** If a human has to pick a folder, the system is broken.
- **Local Sovereignty:** Your vault, your keys, your agent.

## Deployment Strategy
- **Standard:** Localhost-only.
- **Power-User:** Bridge on a headless server + Tailscale.
- **Developer:** Custom templates and skill forks.
