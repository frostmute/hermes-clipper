# 🤝 Community & Inspirations

Hermes Clipper doesn't exist in a vacuum. We are part of a vibrant, rapidly evolving ecosystem of "Second Brain" builders, agentic pioneers, and efficiency hackers. This project is a love letter to the tools and communities that made it possible.

---

## 🏗 Built on the Shoulders of Giants

### [The Obsidian Community](https://obsidian.md)
The foundation of our "Garden." We are inspired by the thousands of plugin developers and thinkers (like **Tiago Forte**, **Nick Milo**, and the **Linking Your Thinking** crew) who turned local Markdown files into a global movement for personal sovereignty.
- **Our Goal:** To complement existing workflows. Whether you use the official Clipper, Dataview, or Smart Connections, we aim to be the "Agentic Glue" that helps organize the gaps in between.

### [The Hermes Agent Ecosystem](https://github.com/frostmute/hermes-agent)
The "Brain" of the operation. Without the core Hermes architecture, this suite would just be another static scraper. We are proud to contribute back to this ecosystem with our specialized clipping logic.

### [The MCP Protocol (Model Context Protocol)](https://modelcontextprotocol.io)
Anthropic's MCP changed the game for how local data talks to LLMs. While Hermes Clipper uses a custom **Bridge Pattern** for remote and mobile-friendly ingestion, we are deeply aligned with the mission of making local vaults accessible to intelligent agents everywhere.

---

## ⚡ Efficiency Pioneers

A core pillar of Hermes Clipper is **Token Sovereignty**. We didn't invent this philosophy—we just obsess over it.

### [Julius Brussee's Caveman Methodology](https://github.com/JuliusBrussee/caveman)
The legendary "Caveman Mode" is the spiritual heart of our efficiency layer. Julius's work on linguistic compression proved that you don't need polite filler to have high-intelligence outcomes. By "Grunting" our data, we slash token costs and increase speed for everyone.

### [The RAG & Local-First Communities](https://localfirstweb.com/)
From the developers of **LangChain** and **LlamaIndex** to the proponents of **Local-First Software**, we take inspiration from everyone working to ensure that AI remains a tool for the individual, not just the cloud.
